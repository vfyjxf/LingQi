<objective>
Rewrite the frontend StatsPanel and its data pipeline to consume AI-generated dimension scores instead of client-side risk-count calculations. This replaces the technical debt calcQualityScore() with AI-derived aggregate scoring and switches the radar chart from "risk count per category" to "quality score per dimension" (DIM-03).

Purpose: The current calcQualityScore() is arbitrary (blocker*20 + major*12 + minor*5 + nit*2) and listed as technical debt in CONCERNS.md. The radar chart shows risk density, not quality. This phase delivers the user-facing value: a 7-axis radar chart showing AI-calibrated quality scores with grade derivation from AI data.
Output: StatsPanel renders AI dimension scores in a green radar chart, grade badge uses AI-derived aggregate, calcQualityScore() is deleted, demo data includes sample dimension scores.
</objective>
